# Collisions in the displaying region

This smart dashboard visualizes reported vehicle collisions within the city limits of Seattle. The map acts as a proportional symbol map, where the size of each point represents the severity or number of vehicles involved in the incident.

The chart dynamically updates to show the breakdown of collision severities based on the current viewport. Data is sourced from the Seattle Open Data Portal.
